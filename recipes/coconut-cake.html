Coconut Cake

Description

The easiest recipe on the planet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self raising flour
  • 1 cup caster sugar
  • (optional) 1 sachet vanilla sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter or oil
  • Springling dough: 1 cup dessicated coconut + 1/2 cup icing sugar
  • Pouring on baked dough: 1-2 bottles heavy cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180-185 Â°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix all ingredients well and form dough.
  3. Pour the dough into a form with a higher top with baking paper.
  4. Sprinkle the dough with a mix of coconut and icing sugar.
  5. Bake for about 25 minutes until golden reddish color.
  6. Immediately after you take the form out of the oven, pour heavy cream evenly to the whole surface so that it soaks into the hot dough.
